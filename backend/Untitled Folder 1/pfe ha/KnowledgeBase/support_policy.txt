Customer Support Policy:

Support Channels:
- Email support
- Phone support

Response Time:
- Emails received during working hours are answered within 2 hours.
- Emails received outside working hours are answered on the next business day.

Technical Issues:
Technical issues are analyzed by the technical team and resolved as quickly as possible depending on complexity.

Service Availability:
Our AI services are available 24/7. However, human support is only available during working hours.

Customer Responsibilities:
Customers are expected to provide clear and accurate information when requesting support.

Data Privacy:
Customer data is handled securely and is not shared with third parties.
